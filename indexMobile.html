WHAT IS UNIONDAO?

A transparent, sustainable financial system that provides secure, stable, mortgage and decentralized governance mechanisms.

WHAT IS THE GOVERNANCE PASS FOR UNIONDAO?

UNIONDAO's governance pass is known as UDAO, which can be used to pay for general equivalent redemption service fees and mortgage interest, and participate in community governance voting. The value of UDAO is directly related to the total value of the UNIONDAO economy.

ABOUT UNIONDAO

100% RESERVE

Any one of the issued stable coins has the equivalent assets as the reserve pledge in the contract.

ADDITIONAL ON-DEMAND

Users can create new currency when the market needs it.

TOTAL CONTROL

The governance system regulates the pledge issuance of each asset and the total amount of system issuance.

FREE USE

The system does not have a freeze or central arbitration mechanism, users control their own assets independently, and private key signature is the only means of transferring assets.

RISK ISOLATION

The value of the stable currency anchors the legal currency,However legal currency reserve is not needed nor does bank account.

AUTOMATIC FORWARDING

The automatic forwarding function can automatically forward the received stable currency to the specified collection address, which is convenient for the exchange or the acquirer to use.

FLEXIBLE AND SECURE STABLE CURRENCY

The system does not freeze and has no central arbitration mechanism. Users control their own assets independently. Private key signature is the only means of transferring assets. Assets are guaranteed to maintain stable value without boundaries and restrictions.

COMMUNITY AUTONOMY

Open community governance, UDAO holders participate in making risk-related decisions to ensure the healthy development of the system.
