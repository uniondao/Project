FAQ

What is UNIONDAO?

A transparent, sustainable financial system that provides secure, stable, mortgage and decentralized governance mechanisms.

What is the governance pass for UNIONDAO?

UNIONDAO's governance pass is known as UDAO, which can be used to pay for general equivalent redemption service fees and mortgage interest, and participate in community governance voting. The value of UDAO is directly related to the total value of the UNIONDAO economy.

What is the stable currency of UNIONDAO?

UNIONDAO's US dollar stable currency is named UNDT (UNION DOLLAR TOKEN), and UNDT value is linked to USDT, PAX, DAI, USDC, TUSD and other anchored US dollar stable currencies. UNDT is a certificate issued by a decentralized smart contract that anchors a basket of US dollar stable coins and is pledged by a 100% reserve on the chain. The value is always equal to one unit of the US dollar stable currency. UNIONDAO will also offer other types of stable coins.

What is the issuance mechanism for the UNDT dollar stable currency?

When a user pledges a unit of system-approved dollar-stable currency to a smart contract, the smart contract automatically casts a unit of UNDT and transfers it to the user.

What is the way UNIONDAO is governed?

All UDAO Certified Card holders vote on matters related to community governance and are implemented on the principle that the minority is subject to majority.
